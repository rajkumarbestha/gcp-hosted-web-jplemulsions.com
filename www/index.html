Our Services

JPL MS Cationic Bitumen Emulsion

For Road Maintenance.

This high quality material makes it possible to fill potholes or carry out patch repair with open-graded cold premix made on-site.

JPL RS-1 Cationic Bitumen Emulsion

For Tack Coating.

Tack Coat Application is a light spray of Bitumin Emulsion, which may be hand or machine sprayed and ensures a good bond between old and new surface layers.

JPL RS-2 Cationic Bitumen Emulsion

For Surface Dressing.

It consists of application in one or two coats, each consisting of a layer of bituminous binder sprayed on a previously prepared base.

JPL SS-1 Cationic Bitumen Emulsion

Application.

This is an absorptive surface material, designed to penetrate, plug capillary voids in the surface, bond and stabilizes the existing surface and to promote adhesion between it and the construction course that follows.

JPL SS-2 Cationic Bitumen Emulsion

Application

Used as a prime coat, this absorptive surface material is designed to penetrate, bond and stabilize the existing surface and to promote adhesion between it and the construction course that follows.
